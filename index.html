Introduction

DesignLab explores critical design approaches for Digital Humanities work through conceptual and applied work. Our goals for DesignLab include:

  • Develop a foundation for imagining, scoping, managing, and developing our own web-based digital work;
  • Learn to use design elements and concepts to review existing digital humanities work and to develop our own work;
  • Learn ways of storytelling using web-based design and development approaches;
  • Inspect and understand source code, as well as manage our own source code;
  • Publish a personal portfolio and a modest prototype for your research project.
